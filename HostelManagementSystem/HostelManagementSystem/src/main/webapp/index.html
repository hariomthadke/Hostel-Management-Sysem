Amenities :

The students are provided with basic amenities like cot, table, chair, cupboard and fan in their rooms. The rooms are spacious, airy and illuminated. Students common rooms/dining halls have TV sets. The 24 hours water supply is provided to all hostels. A generator is being added to ensure supply to messes and common rooms of girls hostel.
